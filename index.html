Roster Advisory Information (Beta Mode)

This tool provides general guidance. It has not been tested on a wide audience and may contain inaccuracies. Always refer to the official EA document and the rostering policy for definitive interpretation.

The tool currently checks for the following potential roster advisories and concerns:

  • **Early Starts Advisory** [EA 14.8]: More than 3 consecutive starts before 06:00. Note: This may be permissible if mutually agreed.
  • **Minimum RDOs** [EA 14.11]: Fewer than 8 Rostered Days Off in a 28-day roster period.
  • **Weekend Off** [EA 14.16]: No full Saturday and Sunday RDO block in a 28-day roster period. Note: This may be permissible if mutually agreed.
  • **Consecutive Duty Tours** [EA 14.15]: Two tours each consisting of 6 or more consecutive duty days without a minimum of 2 consecutive days off in between. Note: This may be permissible if mutually agreed.
  • **Rest Periods (Home Base)** [EA 14.48]: Less than 12 hours rest between duties when both duties are at Home Base.
  • **Short Rest Periods (Info)**: Less than 12 hours rest between duties when one or both duties are away from Home Base. This is for information only as it might be permissible under specific FRMS rules (e.g., 10-hour rule when away).

The tool **does NOT** currently check for:

  • **Grey Day Directed Work**: (e.g., being directed to work on a Grey Day outside of assessment/remedial training, or if you have nominated to work).
  • Specific RDO and Grey Day buffer periods (e.g., 36-hour for 1 RDO, 60-hour for 2 RDOs, etc.).
  • Any Flight Duty Period (FDP) or Duty Time limitations based on CAO 48 or FRMS not explicitly covered above.
  • Maximum consecutive nights away from Home Base.
  • Any specific Rest Periods related to Standby.
  • Any other complex rostering rules or buffers.
